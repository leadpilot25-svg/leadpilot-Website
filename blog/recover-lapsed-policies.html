How to Recover Lapsed Insurance Policies: Script Guide

By LeadPilot Research Team • June 18, 2025 • 6 min read

In the insurance business in India, renewals are an advisor's primary and most stable source of passive income. Every policy renewed is a commission saved. However, clients are busy. They get distracted, forget the grace periods, or miss premium payment alerts. If you aren't tracking renewals proactively, you are leaving thousands on the table.

When a policy lapses, reconnecting with the policyholder can feel awkward. But with the right gentle, helpful tone, you can recover up to 50% of your lapsed book. Here is our step-by-step reactivation guide and the exact copy-paste scripts you should use on WhatsApp today.

Why Do Policies Lapse? (And the Hidden Opportunity)

Most policyholders don't intentionally cancel their policies. More than 70% of lapses in India happen due to:

  • Forgotten grace periods or bank auto-debit failures.
  • Spam folders swallowing premium reminder emails.
  • A slight cash flow crunch at renewal time.

This is where you step in not as a aggressive salesperson, but as a helpful advisor. Highlight that a lapsed health or life policy leaves their family vulnerable to sudden emergencies. Frame your message around protection, safety, and convenience.

Copy-Paste WhatsApp Recovery Scripts

1. The Friendly Reconnect (Use for 1-15 Days Lapsed)

Hi [CLIENT NAME] 👋 A friendly note from [YOUR NAME] — I noticed that your [POLICY NAME] policy has crossed its renewal date. We still have a grace period to pay the premium online without any penalty or medical checkup. It takes less than 2 minutes. Let me know if you need the direct payment link or help with the premium amount! 😊

2. The Protection Alert (Use for 15-45 Days Lapsed)

Hi [CLIENT NAME], hope you are doing well 🙏 Just following up about your premium renewal. Currently, your coverage is inactive. If there is a health emergency, active coverage ensures your family is completely safe from huge hospital bills. I can help submit a renewal request to the company today to activate your policy immediately. Let me know if we can do this. — [YOUR NAME]

How to Avoid Lapses in the First Place

The best way to handle lapsed policies is to prevent them entirely. Keep a dedicated renewals tracker calendar, set auto-alerts 30 days before the expiry date, and tag high-value renewals with a "Hot" status to follow up personally.
